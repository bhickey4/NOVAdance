Meet Our B+ Heroes!

Rebekah

Rebekah was diagnosed in July 2014 with Ewing Sarcoma, a rare bone cancer in children. She had 14 rounds of chemo and her forefoot amputated but through it all she has managed to #BePositive and start high school (where her favorite subject is English) and live her life. Here is a little more about Rebekah!

Age: 15 years old

Favorite super hero: Captain America

Favorite color: Turquoise

Favorite food: Spaghetti and Meatballs

Favorite hobby: Reading

Interests: Reading, Writing, listening to music, looking at Pinterest

How she stays positive:
By living by a famous quote by Rocky Balboa : “The world ain’t all about sunshine and rainbows. It’s a very mean and nasty place, and I don’t care how tough you are, it will beat you to your knees and keep you there permanently if you let it. You, me, or nobody is gonna hit as hard as life. But it ain’t about how hard you hit. It’s about how hard you can get hit and keep moving forward; how much you can take and keep moving forward. That’s how winning it done…”

Jibril (7/26/2007- 2/28/2016)

Jibril, also known as Jibby, was diagnosed with DIPG, a type of brain cancer that is very aggressive, in 2014. Throughout the course of his treatment and fight, Jibby showed everyone how to #BePositive by keeping his spirits up, baking, spending time with his motorcycle club and his siblings, as well as playing with his dog and cat. Here is a little more about Jibby!

Jibby was one of the happiest 8 year olds we’ve had the pleasure of meeting. He loved to spend time with us coloring, eating pizza and listening to music. Some of his favorite topics included the Hulk, dancing, art, acting, dragons, video games, motorcycles, baking, and online shopping.

Jibby was "adopted" by Tri Delta for NOVAdance. Jibby had so much fun coming to campus to make gingerbread houses and hang out with his college friends. He loved getting mail from Tri-Delta and the NOVAdance committee. As much as Jibby loved hanging out with us, we at NOVAdance loved hanging out with him even more. Jibby passed away on February 28th, 2016. We are forever inspired by Jibby's strength, courage, love and life, even through the hardest times. To everyone on NOVAdance, especially Tri-Delta, Jibby will always be our B+ Hero.

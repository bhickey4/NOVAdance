    Morale Committee (MOCO)

    The Morale Committee is the NOVAdance hype team. Moralers electrify our dance marathon by bringing excitement and positivity to our Heroes, their families, and Villanova’s campus, making NOVAdance an unforgettable day for everyone involved. All Villanova students who are passionate about fighting childhood cancer are invited to join our team and promote the B+ message around campus.
    As a member of the Morale Committee, you will be asked to:

    1. Register with the Morale Committee AND as a general NOVAdance participant at NOVAdance.org.

    2. Attend three Morale Committee meetings in the Spring 2017 semester. (Follow NOVAdance on Facebook for up to date information on meetings!!)

    3. Attend all 12 hours of NOVAdance.

    4. Raise $167 in honor of Andrew McDonough’s battle with Leukemia.
      • Your Morale Committee chairs have plenty of fundraising ideas, this goal is completely reachable!
    5. Participate in NOVAdance fundraising/awareness activities on campus.

    General Participants

    The oldest level of involvement in NOVAdance.
    These individuals register online, fundraise by sending the template email to family and friends, and show up on the day of the event!
    NOVAdance could not exist without these individuals.

    Campus Organizations

    1. Register your team and set a team fundraising goal!
    2. Encourage members of your organization to sign up to dance.
    3. Help promote NOVAdance in any way possible including sending out e-mails to family and friends asking for donations.

    Want more information?
    Contact your philanthropy chair or e-mail novadancemarathon@gmail.com.

    Freshmen

    Interested in being involved in a freshman only position within NOVAdance?

    The SIDEKICK program will be accepting applications at the start of each Fall semester. Check back here for updates

    Sidekicks will be responsible for

    1. Being involved in the planning and execution of NOVAdance as an event and as a yearlong fundraising event.
    2. Being present and active during the event.
    3. Promoting the event to your friends, residence hall, and all of Villanova.
    4. Participating in individual fundraising efforts
    5. Ensuring NOVAdance is as successful as possible
    With your help we can make this event a huge success for our B+ Heroes and help in the fight to end childhood cancer!

Ignite Change, Go Nova, Be Positive!

Villanova’s alumni network is one of the top in the nation – for a reason. Our four years at Nova are far too short; there are too many things to learn, fun times to be had, and great causes to rally behind. But fear not: your involvement with Villanova doesn’t have to end after graduation! Join us here at your alma mater in the fight against childhood cancer!

Nova has always been a place where anything was possible. A Villanova student can do anything with the help of fellow Villanovans, both current and graduated. We need your help to end childhood cancer, and make sure that no family has to hear the words “your child has cancer.”


What can YOU do?

NOVAdance is the third largest dance marathon in the country supporting the B+ Foundation, as well as one of the fastest growing organizations on campus. This is your chance to significantly shape the Villanova environment for decades to come. Any contribution makes a huge difference to the families affected, and helps this campus organization to grow and succeed each year, empowering students to make a difference in the world around them. In line with Villanova’s Augustinian core values, Veritas, Unitas, Caritas, Wildcats have always been about giving back to the community in any way we can. Villanova needs your help, and these kids need your help!

Whether you donate, become a sponsor, stay updated by subscribing to our newsletter, or simply help spread the word about NOVAdance, we are so appreciative of anything you can contribute to help these families in need and to help us move one step closer to a world without cancer.


THANK YOU, VILLANOVANS!

For more information on how to get involved, contact our Alumni Relations Chair, Kori Durando, at alumni@novadance.org.
